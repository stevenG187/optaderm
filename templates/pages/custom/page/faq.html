Cationic Hyaluronic Acid (HA) uses advanced encapsulation technology to combine sodium hyaluronate and polyquaternium-10, providing deep hydration, barrier protection, and soothing relief. It helps retain moisture, reduce inflammation, and support quicker healing and less scarring.

Orders are typically delivered within 7 – 10 business days. Actual delivery time depends on shipping distance and may vary. Shipping costs for your area are determined during the checkout process. All payments for purchases must be paid in advance by valid credit card and in Canadian funds.

I want to buy a product as a gift. Do you ship directly to the recipient?

Yes, you may adjust the delivery address of your order during the checkout process. However, Optaderm Products will label the package as “for personal use only” on customs forms and will not use any other description such as “gift.”
