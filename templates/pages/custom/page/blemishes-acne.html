Blemishes & Acne

What are Blemishes and Acne?

Blemishes and acne occur when oil (sebum) from the skinâ€™s glands mixes with dead skin cells, creating a plug that clogs pores. This leads to various types of blemishes, such as blackheads, whiteheads, and in more severe cases, cysts. The immune system responds by sending white blood cells to fight the infection, resulting in pus-filled pimples. Acne and blemishes are common skin conditions that affect people of all ages.

How to Scientifically Treat Blemishes and Acne?

Effective treatment of acne and blemishes requires both preventing pore blockages and reducing inflammation. At Optaderm, we focus on ingredients that target these core issues:

  • Salicylic Acid: A key ingredient that helps dissolve sebum plugs and clear pores, reducing the occurrence of blemishes.
  • Nordihydroguaiaretic Acid and Oleanolic Acid: These ingredients help control oil production and prevent clogged pores.
  • Niacinamide: Reduces inflammation, calms irritated skin, and minimizes the appearance of acne scars.
  • Peptides and Alpha Arbutin: These ingredients help reduce the chances of scarring and post-inflammatory pigmentation.

Maintaining a consistent skincare routine that includes cleansing, toning, exfoliating, and moisturizing is crucial for managing acne-prone skin. Using warm (not hot) water for rinsing is also important to avoid irritating the skin further.
