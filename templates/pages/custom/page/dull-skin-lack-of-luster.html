Dull Skin + Lack of Luster

What is Dull or Lacklustre Skin?

Dull skin lacks the natural glow and radiance that comes from healthy, hydrated skin. When the skin doesn’t reflect light properly, it can appear tired, uneven, and lacklustre. Dull skin is often the result of factors that slow down the skin’s natural renewal process, leaving behind a buildup of dead skin cells.

How to Scientifically Treat Dull and Lacklustre Skin?

To restore brightness and radiance to dull skin, it’s important to focus on removing dead skin cells and replenishing moisture:

  • Exfoliation: Regular exfoliation is key to removing the buildup of dead skin cells that block light from reflecting off the skin’s surface. Optaderm offers both physical and chemical exfoliators that brighten the skin instantly.
  • Antioxidants: Using a treatment like Active C Encapsulated Vitamin C + Peptide Serum helps neutralize free radicals that damage the skin. This serum also boosts collagen production and evens out pigmentation, giving the skin a more youthful glow.
  • Hydration: Restoring the skin’s water and oil balance is crucial for a radiant complexion. Optaderm’s ceramide-rich moisturizers, such as the Integrity Ceramide Series, improve the skin’s ability to retain moisture and enhance light reflection, giving the skin an instant glow.
