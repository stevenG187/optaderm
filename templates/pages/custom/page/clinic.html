Caitlin

Caitlin has been an esthetician for 17 years. She is a graduate of the Blanche MacDonald Institute in Vancouver, British Columbia with additional studies in the field of Oncology Esthetics. Additionally she completed her Palomar-Cynosure laser training in 2019. Caitlin has been with Optaderm for six years, offering a full range of esthetic and laser therapies. Over the years, she has also taught esthetics at the John Casablanca Institute. Driven by a passion for continuous learning in skin care products, services, and laser technology, Caitlin consistently updates her knowledge and expertise. As part of Optaderm's management team, she oversees the ongoing training of all staff.

Having been with Optaderm for 35 years, she practices a full range of esthetic and laser treatments, and has also operated her own skincare practice in North Vancouver. Nasrin is highly engaged with innovations in the skincare industry, continually updating her knowledge and skills. As a member of Optaderm’s management team, her extensive expertise is a valuable resource to the entire team.
