Eczema

What is Eczema?

Eczema, also known as atopic dermatitis, is a chronic skin condition that causes inflamed, itchy, and irritated skin. It often appears as dry, scaly patches, thickened areas, or even open lesions. Eczema can affect people of all ages, and while the exact cause is not fully understood, it is believed to be related to an overactive immune system and a genetic predisposition.

How to Scientifically Manage Symptoms of Eczema?

  • Moisturization: Consistent moisturizing is critical for eczema-prone skin. Products like Comfort Facial Balm and Repair Lipid Ceramide Barrier Serum offer long-lasting hydration and improve the skin’s resilience.
  • Avoid Harsh Conditions: Reducing exposure to UV rays, using warm (not hot) water for cleansing, and protecting the skin from heat and cold can minimize flare-ups.
